Green Fantasy Forest

  • Located:
    No. 7, Jianwo, Gaopu Village, Xihu Township, Miaoli County , Taiwan (R.O.C.)
  • Ticket price:
    Three hours:$300
    ALL day:$500
  • Opening hours:
    09:00-18:00
  • Introduction:
    After visiting beautiful landscapes around the world, a group of photographers who love capturing moments wanted to recreate the emotions they experienced during their journeys in Taiwan. Thus, they built the "Green Enchanted Forest," a park that combines natural ecology with a touch of fairy tale whimsy.
    The park features various Instagram-worthy attractions, including a Harry Potter Magic House, a Hobbit Village from the Lord of the Rings, exotic architecture, a water church, an Indian campsite, and a French vineyard garden. It allows visitors to travel around the world without leaving the country, making it a popular choice for wedding photoshoots. Regular visitors can pay an admission fee to explore the unique buildings and landscapes. However, if you plan to have a wedding photoshoot here, there are special pricing and arrangements. Please check the official website for more information.

Xinshe Castle Summit Resort

  • Located:
    No. 23, Xiezhong St., Xinshe Dist., Taichung City , Taiwan (R.O.C.)
  • Opening hours:
    Monday to Friday:09:00~18:00
    Saturday and Sunday:08:00~18:00
  • Ticket price：
    Full price ticket:$250
    Half-price ticket.:$150
  • Introduction:
    Located in the Dakan Scenic Area, "Sinseh Villa" offers breathtaking views from the moment you step into the estate. It is filled with stunning sights such as magnificent castles, Roman arches, elegant bridges, and wooden terraces by the pond. The entire estate exudes a rich European style that is loved by visitors from all over. It is also a popular destination for wedding photoshoots.

Tonight Starry Sky Leisure Farm

  • Located:
    No. 428, Sec. 1, Shipai Rd., Changhua City, Changhua County , Taiwan (R.O.C.)
  • Opening hours：
    Monday to Friday:10:00~17:00
    Saturday and Sunday:9:00~17:00
    Closed on Tuesdays.
  • Ticket price：
    Full price ticket:$200（Some items in the park can be discounted by NT$50.)
    Concession ticket:$60 (Children below 140 centimeters in height.)
    Free:
    1. Fangyuan Township, Changhua County (restricted to residents of Caohu Village).
    2. Shihpai Village, Changhua City (local residents only).
    3. Holders of disability certificates.
  • Introduction:
    Located in Changhua City, the "Tonight Starry Leisure Farm" offers a tranquil and relaxing environment. It is conveniently situated near the high-speed rail line, allowing visitors to catch a glimpse of the speeding trains. The farm is surrounded by lush greenery and adorned with blooming flowers throughout the seasons. Inside the farm, various adorable animals are raised, providing children with an opportunity to learn about different species. Visitors can also enjoy up-close interactions with the animals, such as feeding and petting them. The farm features picturesque landscapes that are perfect for taking photos and creating memorable moments. On Saturdays and Sundays, the farm is bustling with visitors eager to explore and enjoy the farm's offerings.

The Yawen Bali Coast Tourism Factory

  • Located:
    No. 333, Zhongxing Rd., Douliu City, Yunlin County , Taiwan (R.O.C.)
  • Opening hours:
    8:30~17:00
  • Introduction:
    The Yawen Bali Coast Tourist Factory boasts an impressive and spacious area. The large park encompasses over ten different themed factory zones, allowing visitors to immerse themselves in the rich and diverse ambiance of the Nanyang region without the need to travel abroad.
    Yawen Bali Coast, the largest Nanyang-themed attraction in Taiwan, is renowned for its production of the popular Yawen Cypress Aromatherapy Transparent Soap. It offers a perfect blend of relaxation, art, aesthetics, and shopping, making it a prime destination for tourists. Visitors can learn about the soap-making process while immersing themselves in the Nanyang ambiance, enjoying a leisurely and pleasant experience.

Tiebilun Waterfall

  • Located:
    No. 20, Provincial Highway 14A, Ren'ai Township, Nantou County , Taiwan (R.O.C.)
  • Introduction:
    Located at an elevation of approximately 1,300 meters in the western part of Ren'ai Township, Nantou County, is the Red Fragrance Tribe. The steep black canyon walls of the Tibeiren Gorge are adorned with vibrant orange, yellow, and white streaks caused by the long-term oxidation of minerals. The greenery adds a touch of beauty, creating a colorful valley. The rushing water has carved out beautiful curved lines, forming a magnificent and breathtaking gorge. Due to its remote location and difficult access by vehicles, the area remains relatively untouched, maintaining its pristine forest scenery. It truly is a natural masterpiece worth exploring!
