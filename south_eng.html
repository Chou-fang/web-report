Taiping Suspension Bridge

  • Located:
    No. 5, Xiakengzai, Meishan Township, Chiayi County , Taiwan (R.O.C.)
  • Ticket price:
    Ticket Reservation Method: Online ticket purchase and on-site ticket sales. Full Price:$100
    Discounted ticket:$80
    half-price ticket:$50
    voucher ticket:$30
  • Opening hours:
    Monday to Friday:09:00 ~ 16:30
    Saturday and Sunday:09:00 ~ 17:30
    Closed on Wednesdays.
    Starry Night Time: Every Saturday evening from 18:00 to 20:00.
  • Introduction:
    The Taiping Suspension Bridge has a length of 281 meters and is located at an altitude of approximately 1000 meters. It spans across the San-Yuan Rainbow Waterfall, connecting Meishan Taiping Village to Guishan. The bridge offers visitors a panoramic view of the beautiful scenery of the Jiannan Plain and the unique phenomenon of sea of clouds. Source: Tourism Bureau, MOTC, Alishan National Scenic Area Administration.

Siziwan

  • Located:
    Gushan Dist., Kaohsiung City , Taiwan (R.O.C.)
  • Introduction:
    Xiziwan is renowned for its beautiful sunset views and natural rock formations. The area includes attractions such as Xiziwan Beach, Seaside Park, and the former British Consulate at Dagou. Visitors can enjoy breathtaking ocean views and admire the panorama of Kaohsiung Harbor. The beach at Xiziwan exudes a tropical ambiance and a touch of the southern charm. As night falls, with the radiant glow of the sunset and the flickering lights of fishing boats, the scenery becomes a picturesque blend of sea and sky. Xiziwan's sunset is one of the eight scenic wonders of Kaohsiung, and the stone railings along the coastal embankment provide the best vantage point to appreciate the afterglow of the setting sun.

Pier 2 Art Center

  • Located:
    No. 1, Dayong Rd., Yancheng Dist., Kaohsiung City , Taiwan (R.O.C.)
  • Introduction:
    The Pier-2 Art Center, located next to the harbor, is a warehouse complex that has been transformed into an art district. In addition to vehicles, there is also maritime traffic, creating a lively atmosphere complemented by the sea breeze. The vast expanse of the ocean and sky provides a boundless view that reflects our limitless thoughts and aspirations.
    The public sector responsible for the operation and management of the district, along with the imaginative artists, each embrace their own dreams. However, in this space, they coordinate their steps, adjust their directions, and strive together towards a shared ideal. Despite the conflicts between administration and art, they become close partners, constructing the image of the Pier-2 Art Center in the hearts of each individual.


Paradise Of Deer

  • Located:
    No. 1097-1, Henggong Rd., Hengchun Township, Pingtung County , Taiwan (R.O.C.)
  • Ticket price:
    Full price ticket:$200
    Group ticket:$180
    Concession ticket:$50
  • Introduction:
    In southern Taiwan, there are three well-known deer parks: Lu Jing, Ah Xin Chocolate Farm, and Ken Tsao Qu Ecological Park. Let's start with Lu Jing Deer Ecological Park, also known as "Deer Kingdom." Lu Jing is located on Pinggo Highway in Hengchun, Pingtung. It is not far from Hengchun Old Town and Xiao Du's Steamed Buns, only about a 5-minute drive. It is one of the must-visit attractions when traveling to Kenting! You can not only interact with the deer up close and feed them but also take adorable selfies with the cute little deer. You're guaranteed to capture unforgettable moments! The park is clean and spacious, with many Instagrammable walls and art installations for taking beautiful photos. It is also a great place to bring children for a fun day out, making it a perfect destination for family trips.
