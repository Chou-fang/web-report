Northern Taiwan

Including Taipei City, New Taipei City, Keelung City, Yilan County, Taoyuan City, Hsinchu County, and Hsinchu City, these seven cities and counties form the northern region of Taiwan. It is the most populous area in Taiwan and also receives the highest amount of rainfall in the country.

Central Taiwan

Including Miaoli County, Taichung City, Changhua County, Nantou County, and Yunlin County, these five cities and counties are located in the heartland of Taiwan. The climate in this region is comfortable throughout the year, especially in Taichung City, where the temperature is pleasant in all seasons. It is an ideal destination for travel.

Southern Taiwan

Including Chiayi County, Chiayi City, Tainan City, Kaohsiung City, and Pingtung County, these areas form the southern region of Taiwan. The climate here is characterized by summer-like conditions throughout the year. The predominant terrain consists of flat plains, earning it the nickname "Granary of Taiwan".。

Eastern Taiwan

Eastern Taiwan, also known as East Taiwan, refers to the eastern half of Taiwan's territory. It is separated from the western side by the Xueshan and Central Mountain Ranges, while the eastern side faces the Pacific Ocean. It occupies approximately one-third of the total land area of Taiwan. From north to south, it includes Hualien County and Taitung County.
